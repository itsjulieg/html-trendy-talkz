TV Shows and Movies

Introduction

One of the best things to do during the fall season is to get comfy and watch a good TV show or movie. There are so many to choose from, whether you love getting the spooks, or if you just want to get nice and cozy. Our top picks are listed below, so get your warm blankets, pumpkin flavored drinks, and fuzzy socks ready!

TV Shows

One of the most famous TV Shows for the fall season is Gilmore Girls, which is about a mother-daughter bond between Rory Gilmore and her single mother Lorelai Gilmore and their life in their town called Stars Hollow. This show includes themes such as family dynamics, relationships, ambition, and financial troubles. Other TV Shows that are perfect for the fall season include Friends, Twilight, Pretty Little Liars, and Glee.

If you’re looking for some new releases, the fifth Season of Stranger Things is your best bet! Stranger Things is another popular TV Show on Netflix that was released on July 15, 2016, and since then, it has been a major hit! It has an interesting plot about a kid who gets kidnapped and sent to a parallel universe called the Upside Down. It is up to his friends, police officers, and a girl with supernatural powers to not only find him, but to uncover a bunch of secrets and horrors of their hometown of Hawkins, Indiana. It is set to release its new and final season coming out on November 26, 2025, and it’ll end on New Year’s Eve. We’d say that’s the perfect way to end the year!

Movies

Get ready for another new release called Wicked: For Good, the sequel to Wicked. The trilogy is based off the famous musical with the same name. The first movie was “wickedly” successful, earning about $756 million in the Box Office. The sequel is guaranteed to be a success as well, as the ending to the full Wicked plot will unfold.

If you enjoy classic movies, here of list of some you’ll enjoy:

  • Hocus Pocus
  • Beetlejuice
  • Coraline
  • Corpse Bride
  • Nightmare Before Christmas
  • The Breakfast Club
  • The Perks of Being a Wallflower

Conclusion

If you’re up for the latest new TV Shows and Movies, Stranger Things season 5 and Wicked: For Good are just right for you. Classic fall movies are totally in this fall season, so don’t worry about keeping up because the reminiscence of class fall movies will never go out of style. Whether you enjoy spooky movies or comfy and cozy ones, you are sure to be entertained this year!
